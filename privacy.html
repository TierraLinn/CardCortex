Privacy notice

Plain-language launch draft for the CardCortex prototype.

Prototype data

This local prototype uses sample card data and browser-based demo interactions. It does not currently send your card scans, collection records, or chat messages to a production backend.

Camera and uploads

The scanner page may ask for camera permission in your browser. In this prototype, camera and upload activity is used only to demonstrate the scanning flow on your device.

Future account data

A production version may store account details, card photos, collection records, valuation history, grading reports, and selling preferences. Those features should include account controls, deletion options, and secure storage before public launch.
