Privacy notice

Plain-language launch draft for the CardCortex multi-user app.

Account data

CardCortex uses Supabase Auth, per-user database rows, and Supabase Storage for signed-in vault records and card images. Signed-out visitors may see sample card data as a preview, but signed-in accounts show only their own saved collection.

Camera and uploads

The scanner page may ask for camera permission in your browser. Camera and upload activity is processed in the browser first, then saved to your Supabase vault only when you are signed in and choose to save it.

Payments and usage

Paid plan status and monthly usage counters are stored in Supabase. Stripe checkout and webhook events update entitlements for the signed-in user.
