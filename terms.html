Terms of use

Launch draft for the CardCortex prototype.

Prototype only

CardCortex is currently a prototype. Scanner matches, price estimates, AI grades, certification numbers, and selling recommendations are demonstration outputs until real services are connected.

No official grading

AI pre-grading is not a replacement for PSA, BGS, CGC, SGC, or another official grading provider. Any certification preview shown by CardCortex is internal to the app unless a real grading workflow is later approved and launched.

Pricing and selling

Card values can change quickly. Marketplace routes and price ranges should be treated as research aids, not guaranteed sale prices or financial advice.

Marketplace relationships

CardCortex is not currently affiliated with eBay, TCGPlayer, Cardmarket, COMC, or other marketplaces named in the prototype.
